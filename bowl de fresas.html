    STRAWBERRIES BOWL

  • It is a clear cup filled with fresh sliced strawberries as the base, layered around the sides so they are visible from the outside.
  • The strawberries are covered with a rich, sweet chocolate sauce that creates a marbled look inside the cup.
  • It is topped with a generous layer of smooth, fluffy whipped cream.
  • There are extra fresh strawberry pieces placed on top for added freshness and natural sweetness.
  • The combination creates a creamy, cold, and very sweet dessert that feels indulgent and refreshing at the same time.
  • It is usually served chilled and can include different toppings depending on your preference.
