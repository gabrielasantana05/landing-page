PISTACHIO STRAWBERRIES

  • It is a bowl with fresh whole strawberries as the base
  • The strawberries are covered with a rich pistachio sauce made with chocolate white Italian and pistachio flavor.
  • There is crushed pistachio on top for extra nutty crunch.
  • It includes kataifi (crispy shredded pastry) baked for added texture.
  • The pistachio sauce gives it a nutty, creamy, and sweet flavor that is different from just plain strawberries with chocolate
  • It doesnt usually have added cream or ice cream unless you order extra toppings
