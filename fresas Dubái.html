    DUBÁI STRAWBERRIES

  • It is a clear cup filled with fresh whole and sliced strawberries as the base, layered so you can see them through the sides.
  • The strawberries are covered with a thick, creamy pistachio sauce that gives the dessert a smooth green color.
  • There is a drizzle of chocolate sauce layered inside and on top for extra sweetness.
  • It is topped with crushed pistachios and crispy shredded pastry (kataifi) for added crunch and texture.
  • Fresh whole strawberries are placed on top, making it look rich and colorful.
  • The combination of pistachio cream, chocolate, and crunchy toppings gives it a nutty, creamy, sweet, and slightly crispy texture.
