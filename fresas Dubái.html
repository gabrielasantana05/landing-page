• Fresas pistacho
  • Bowl de fresas
  • The base is usually fresh strawberries arranged in a bowl
  • It has a semi-sweet chocolate coating or drizzle over the strawberries
  • Often includes pistachio pieces or pistachio cream for a nutty flavor
  • It is topped with kataifi (shredded crisp pastry) or crunchy elements
  • The bowl can come in different sizes like 12 oz or 16 oz
  • It is usually more expensive than simple fruit bowls because of the toppings and chocolate.
